Biography

Charles Michael "Chuck" Palahniuk, born February 21, 1962, is an American novelist known for his transgressive fiction. He has authored 19 novels, including the critically acclaimed *Fight Club*, which was adapted into a major film.

Before becoming a writer, he worked as a journalist and volunteered at homeless shelters, where his experiences inspired many of his works.
